About The Fund

Founded in 20XX, the Berkeley SIF operates with the discipline and structure of a real-world asset manager. Our goal is twofold: to deliver superior risk-adjusted returns and to develop the next generation of finance professionals.

Hands-On Portfolio Management

Members actively research, pitch, and vote on potential investments for our multi-sector equity portfolio. This is not a simulation; decisions impact real capital.

Rigor & Training

Our structured training program covers essential skills from DCF modeling and comparable analysis (comps) to advanced portfolio construction and risk mitigation techniques.

The Cal Network Advantage

We leverage our strong alumni ties to host exclusive speaker events, mentorship opportunities, and career treks to financial centers like San Francisco and New York.

Our Investment Strategy

Value-Oriented Approach

We seek out fundamentally undervalued companies with strong balance sheets and sustainable competitive advantages (moats) for long-term growth.

Bottom-Up Research

Decisions are driven by proprietary, intensive sector research, expert interviews, and sophisticated financial modeling, including sensitivity analysis.

Disciplined Risk Control

We maintain low portfolio turnover and utilize defined hedging strategies to minimize exposure and preserve capital across all market cycles.
